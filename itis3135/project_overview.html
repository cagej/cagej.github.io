Project Outline

Project Overview

  • The application provides a summary of the company and what they do. The purpose is to provide people unfamiliar with the company an insight into their services or to contact the company with their needs.
  • People who need things taken care of.
  • The website will include a company about, drop-off locations, contact information, job application, and routes.

Site Map

Page Design

Home

  • The purpose of this page is to introduce users to the site and explain what the company does and offers.
  • The audience is new and returning customers.
  • The content of the page will be describing the company’s operations and then directing users to where they need to go.
  • No data will be entered on this page.
  • The page will have a header that leads users to other pages.

About

  • The purpose is to give a company history and showcase what the company can do/offers.
  • The audience is new customers or customers looking for a service.
  • This page will have the owner’s information alongside the company’s story and mission statement.
  • No data will be entered on this page.
  • Hyperlinks will take users to subpages detailing the history and mission.
  • Users will be taken to subpages upon clicking a link.

Drop-off Locations

  • This page details drop-off locations.
  • The audience is new users.
  • The page shows drop-off location’s addresses and hours.
  • No validation needed.
  • There will be a button to show location on google maps.
  • Once the button is clicked it takes you to a google map view of the location.

Contact Information

  • Purpose is to provide customers with contact information if needed.
  • New and returning customers.
  • Will have the company address, email, and phone number.
  • No data entered.
  • Button to show the company on map.
  • Once clicked the company location will appear on google maps.

Job Application

  • To allow users to apply for jobs.
  • New users.
  • Will have position openings listed.
  • If there is an opening you’ll be asked to submit your application online.
  • The input will need to be sanitized.
  • This page contains buttons.
  • If you click the application link you’ll be taken to a page where you can submit your information and resume.

Routes

  • Purpose is displaying routes.
  • New customers.
  • Will have the route locations and schedule.
  • No data entered.
  • No buttons or hyperlinks.
  • No actions will occur, this page is view only.

Dynamic Functionality

Page will include header/footer. Will include a form on the application page like this. Interactive images will be displayed on the drop-off and contact pages to help customers better locate the site location like this.
