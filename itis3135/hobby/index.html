About Us

Who are we? We are a group dedicated to bringing F1 fans all the information needed for them to have a great experience at the track. We highlight the best seats at the best tracks to ensure you know exactly where to see all the action. Our team have been to every F1 circuit on the calendar and have gathered the data to share with you!

We have been going to races for over 15 years and have experienced many seating remodels and additions. We have decided to pool our knowledge together so new race-goers can have the best time possible. Our website is a hobby project so no donations or ads are required to keep it running.

Our Favorites To Attend: Monaco, Hungary, Belgium, Canada

Our Favorite Tracks and Why:

Monaco: One of motorsports most iconic races, Monaco is one of F1's oldest races. Today's modern cars may make it near impossible to pass and the racing has certainly gone down over the years, but there's always more to this weekend than the race. Parties happening throughout the city alongside the casinos, Monaco has plenty to keep you up all week and a race I think should be on every F1 fans bucket list. Best seat: Grandstand K Upper or your yacht

Hungary: Excellent views, affordable ticket prices, warm weather, and proximity to Budapest all make the Hungaroring one of the best places to attend a race. I've been to this race on three seperate occasions and have never been disappointed. Side note: Budapest itself is one of my favorite european cities to visit. Best seat: Grandstands Gold 4

Belgium: Another legendary track, Spa offers some of the most challenging racing all year. The track is surrounded by nature and has good track views, however, the transportation is a bit challenging if you decide to commute to and from the track. Best seat: Grandstands Gold 4

Canada: In my opinion, the best NA race to see in-person. One metro stop from downtown Montreal, the race is well-organized with reasonable ticket prices. Montreal has plenty to do on race week and won't cost an arm and leg like other NA races. However, rising crowds recently have led to congession on the bridges and walkways. Best seat: Grandstands 11 and 12
