Welcome to Barone Sanitation

Barone Sanitation is the largest waste management site in the greater NJ area. We offer garbage pickup and dropoff, click to find out if your address is within reach for our services. We are open from 4am-2pm Monday-Friday and 6am-4pm Saturday-Sunday. For phone or email information visit the Contact page. Applications are available online in the Application page.

About

Barone Sanitation has been proudly owned and operated by the Barone family since 1930. Barone Sanitation can take care of any trash no matter how big or messy. We've been voted New Jersey's best sanitation site fifteen times over the last 40 years. Dick himself has worked in waste management since 1972 and is proud to serve the greater NJ area.
